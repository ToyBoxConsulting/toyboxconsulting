About Katoya

I've lived both sides of the systems I now build.

I'm Katoya Raquell Palmer — founder and principal of ToyBox Consulting & Management. A systems strategist and operations executive with 20+ years of cross-sector leadership, I build the finance, governance, and accountability infrastructure that lets mission-driven organizations keep their promises.

From Section 8 to the C-suite — that range is the whole point.
The throughline

I've held a Section 8 voucher and I've sat in the COO's chair of a $12M organization. That range isn't a line on a résumé — it's the reason I can see where an organization is fragile before it breaks.

I founded ToyBox in 2008 and have led operations across budgets from $300K to $24M. Most recently, as COO of a $12M social-justice nonprofit with 80+ staff, I rebuilt all five core systems — finance, HR, IT, grants, and communications — during organizational crisis, and stabilized a team on the edge of collapse.

I work at the intersection of structure and soul: systems that don't just satisfy a funder's checklist, but actually make the work stronger and the people steadier.

“Inclusion is not charity. It is infrastructure.”
