Browser-Level Visitor Protections

We use a layered set of browser security mechanisms to protect visitors using our Sites. These measures do not collect any new data — they restrict what the browser will do on our pages.

  • Content-Security-Policy (CSP) restricts the sources from which scripts, styles, frames, and other resources may load. This blocks injected malicious scripts and limits third-party data exposure.
  • frame-ancestors directive prevents any other site from embedding our pages in a hidden iframe (clickjacking protection).
  • Permissions-Policy explicitly disables browser APIs our pages do not need: camera, microphone, geolocation, payment, USB, MIDI, motion sensors, ambient light, autoplay, encrypted media, screen capture, and picture-in-picture. Even if our site were compromised, an attacker could not use it to request access to your camera or microphone.
  • Referrer-Policy: strict-origin-when-cross-origin limits what URL information is shared with third-party services (Calendly, Crisp, embedded content) when a visitor follows a link away from our Sites.
  • HTTPS-only with HSTS enforces encrypted connections between you and our Sites.
  • Bot protection via Cloudflare Turnstile on all form submissions reduces spam and automated abuse.
