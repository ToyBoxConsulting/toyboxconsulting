The details

What we ask — and when.

The application collects

A snapshot of your firm — nothing you don't already have on hand.
  • Company name, primary contact, and how to reach you
  • Location, year established, and business structure
  • Certifications (MWBE, 8(a), HUBZone, DBE, and others)
  • Primary capability areas and how you like to engage
  • Typical rate range and insurance status
  • SAM.gov / UEI, if you have one
  • Three project examples and a capability statement

Before any contract

Not required to apply — required once we're ready to work together.
  • Certificate of Insurance — general liability and professional / E&O
  • Valid business license & registration (WA UBI if applicable)
  • W-9 on file
  • Signed mutual NDA / subcontractor agreement
  • SAM.gov registration — only if the engagement is federally funded

SAM.gov registration is free and speeds federal and state eligibility — advised, not required.
