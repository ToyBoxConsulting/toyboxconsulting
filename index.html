About Katoya

Two decades building the systems that hold organizations together.

I founded ToyBox in 2008 and have led operations from $300K startups to $24M systems — most recently as COO of a $12M nonprofit with 80+ staff, rebuilt during crisis. I'm a published researcher, the author of the Five Windows essay series, and currently serve on four boards.
