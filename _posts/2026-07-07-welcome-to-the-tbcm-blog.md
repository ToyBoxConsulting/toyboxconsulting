I rebuilt my firm's entire operating stack this spring — three websites migrated off Wix, a full email-auth chain restored, consent-gated analytics on every page, and an agent that reads and writes the 20 workbooks that run the business. Most of that work happened quietly.

This blog is where the consulting work gets documented: operations infrastructure for nonprofits and small teams, board governance in practice, and the build logs behind the systems I use with clients.

If you run a mission-driven organization and the infrastructure is the thing slipping, this is written for you.
