What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file a website stores on your device. We also use similar technologies (local storage, session storage, web beacons / pixels, tracking scripts). For simplicity we call them all "cookies." Some are deleted when you close the browser ("session cookies"); others stay for a set period ("persistent cookies").

Embedded third-party content

When you interact with the following embedded or linked services, those providers set their own cookies under their own policies:

  • Calendly — scheduling
  • Eventbrite — RSVP / event registration
  • Substack — newsletter subscription
  • Vimeo — embedded video

These cookies load only after you interact with the embedded element or follow the link.

How to manage cookies

  • On this site: Use the "Open cookie settings" button above or the "Cookie settings" link in the footer.
  • In your browser: All major browsers allow you to view, block, or delete cookies. See your browser's help pages.
  • Global Privacy Control (GPC): We honor the GPC signal as an opt-out of "sharing" for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • Google Analytics: install the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.
  • Microsoft: opt out at choice.microsoft.com.
  • RB2B: opt out at app.retention.com/optout.
